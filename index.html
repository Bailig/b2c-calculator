The formulas

LTV = AOV × margin × freq × lifespan
CAC = cost per lead ÷ close rate
Ratio = LTV ÷ CAC
Payback = CAC ÷ (AOV × margin × freq ÷ 12)
Max CAC = LTV ÷ 3

How to read the ratio

  • 3× +Healthy. Scale your best acquisition channels confidently.
  • 1.5 – 3×Marginal. Profitable, but don't increase spend until the ratio improves.
  • Below 1.5×Stop spending. Fix AOV, margin, or close rate before acquiring more leads.

What moves the needle most

  • 01Raising close rate from 8% → 15% cuts your CAC nearly in half — often faster than cutting CPL.
  • 02Repeat bookings compound LTV dramatically. Even one extra trip per year can push a marginal ratio into healthy territory.
  • 03Low-CPL referral channels (hotel concierge, travel advisors) can produce the same customer at 5–10× lower CAC than paid ads.
